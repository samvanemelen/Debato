Steem tokens are the main token of the Steem blockchain. it is a liquid currency that can be powered up into Steem Power, it can be transferred to other users or traded for SBD or fiat currencies. The price fluctuates like any other coin.

Steem Tokens (STEEM)

Steem Power is a measurement of the influence you have in the Steem Network. The more power a user holds, the higher his upvotes are valued. It depends on the amount of VESTS a user has compared to the amount of VESTS present on the blockchain.

This measure is not liquid, you cannot trade this directly. You need to 'power down' your Steem Power which converts it to liquid Steem over the course of 3 months.

You can however delegate Steem Power to other users. This allows the other user to have higher valued votes. You can remove delegations at any time and your SP is returned after a period of one week.

Steem Power (SP)

Steem Backed Dollars are a currency designed to be pegged to the US Dollar. it is, just as Steem a liquid token and can be used for trading.

Steem Backed Dollars (SBD)

Savings are a way to protect your precious coins from hackers. Funds in the savings are subjected to a 3-day waiting period. If someone else would gain access to your account, he would have to wait 3 days before those funds become available. This should give you enough time to change your passwords and lock out any unwanted access.
